# El Fierro del Año - Sponsorship Information

## Event Overview
- **Name**: El Fierro del Año (Recognizing True Heroes of the Road)
- **Organizer**: The Truck Savers
- **Website**: www.thetrucksavers.com/elfierrodelano
- **Contact Email**: info@lostrucksavers.com

## Event Description
- The first and only awards for the best of social networks in the transportation industry
- "The Greatest of the Year"
- Awards prizes for anecdotes from the lives of truckers
- Topics: insecurity, poorly paid loads, unscrupulous brokers, tow trucks, love stories on the road, family stories, creativity and humor from the cabs

## Mission
A gathering to recognize the sacrifice and effort of the trucking community that keeps the transportation industry moving.

The Truck Savers promotes:
- Unity of the Latino trucking community
- Recognition and respect of this noble profession
- Cooperation in the repair and strengthening of the industry
